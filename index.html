Mono
Overview Applets Roadmap

A utility suite for
focused, distraction-free
workflows.

Mono is a minimalist, modular app launcher for Apple devices, inspired by the focused aesthetic of E-Ink. Built in Swift with SwiftUI, Mono offers a quiet, clutter-free home screen and curated tools that stay out of your way.

Download on App Store

NEW IN v1.1.0

  • Weather Applet - Real-time conditions from NWS with temp, humidity, wind, and UV index.
  • RSS Reader - Auto-fetch feeds, dedicated refresh button, cleaner design.
  • What's New Screen - See feature highlights when the app updates.

// DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Minimalist

Monospaced UI with a pure black-and-white palette.

Low-Stimulation

Little to no motion, color, or haptics.

Modular

Every feature lives in its own Swift Package.

E-Ink Inspired

High-contrast layouts that reduce visual noise.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Mono stores all user data directly on your device. We do not transmit or store personal data on our servers.

Location Data (Weather Applet)

The Weather applet uses precise location to fetch weather from the National Weather Service (api.weather.gov). Coordinates are sent directly to NWS and not stored elsewhere.

Analytics

No analytics or third-party tracking. Everything stays local.
